We meet monthly for a few drinks, some food & some chat. It's better than it sounds.

How about an evening of talks?

Where?

The inaugural Refresh Glasgow talks took place in Sloans, Argyle Street, Glasgow.

When?

The talks took place on Wednesday 20th February.

Speakers

Our first speakers were: Neil Scott [twitter | web] & Gavin Montague [twitter | web].

Neil's talk was titled Drawing the line: the ethics of web design & delves into understanding why we draw the line where we do and what the implications are for our portfolios.

Gavin's talk was titled Email for web apps & covers the bare minimum all developers should know about email.

Tickets

Talks are free but ticketed to control numbers.

Talks are announced on twitter and via an email list - if you wish to know about future events, follow us on twitter @refreshgla or sign up to the email list below.
