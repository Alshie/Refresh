{Refresh Glasgow}

Refresh Glasgow is a meetup of designers, developers and people who work on the web.

We meet monthly for a few drinks, some food & some chat. It's better than it sounds.

Sounds good, how do I get involved?

Where?

The next Refresh Glasgow will take place in The Arches Cafe Bar, Argyle Street, Glasgow. Just beside the back door of Central Station.

When?

Refresh Glasgow takes place on the third Wednesday of the month. The next pub meet is on 20th March as we are running talks in February, more information below.

How about an evening of talks?

Where?

The inaugural Refresh Glasgow talks will take place in Sloans, Argyle Street, Glasgow. Enter via Argyle Arcade or off the lane on Buchanan Street.

When?

The talks will take place on Wednesday 20th February. Doors open at 1900, talks begin at 1930 sharp. There will be time afterwards for discussion & drinks.

Speakers

Our first speakers are: Neil Scott [twitter | web] & Gavin Montague [twitter | web].

Neil's talk is titled Drawing the line: the ethics of web design & delves into understanding why we draw the line where we do and what the implications are for our portfolios.

Gavin's talk is titled Email for web apps & covers the bare minimum all developers should know about email.

Tickets

The event is free, but is ticketed. Tickets are limited & strictly first come, first served. Tickets will be available Monday 21st Janauary. Ticket sales will be announced via twitter and email list.
